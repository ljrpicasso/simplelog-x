White Dragon, Mature adult

Huge Dragon

Hit Dice: 21d12+105 (241 hp)

Initiative: +4

Speed: 60 ft., burrow 30 ft., fly 200 ft. (poor), swim 60 ft.

Armor Class: 28 (-2 size, +20 natural), touch 8, flat-footed 28

Base Attack/Grapple: +21/+37

Attack: Bite +28 (2d8+8) melee

Full Attack: Bite +28 (2d8+8) melee, 2 Claws +28 (2d6+4) melee, 2 Wings +27 (1d8+4) melee, Tail Slap +27 (2d6+12) melee

Space/Reach: 15 ft./10 ft. (15 ft. with bite)

Special Attacks: Breath weapon (50 ft. cone of cold 7d6, DC 25), crush 2d8+12 (DC 25), frightful presence (DC 21)

Special Qualities: Blindsense 60 ft., damage reduction 10/magic, darkvision 120 ft., fog cloud, gust of wind, icewalking, immunity to cold, immunity to sleep and paralysis, keen senses, spell resistance 20, spells (caster level 3rd), vulnerability to fire

Saves: Fort +17, Ref +12, Will +13

Abilities: Str 27, Dex 10, Con 21, Int 12, Wis 13, Cha 12

Skills: Concentration +13, Diplomacy +9, Escape Artist +8, Hide +0, Intimidate +9, Knowledge (any 1) +25, Listen +27, Move Silently +8, Search +25, Sense Motive +9, Spot +27, Swim +16, Use Magic Device +9

Feats: Alertness, Empower Spell, Flyby Attack, Hover, Improved Initiative, Power Attack, Weapon Focus (bite), Weapon Focus (claw)

Environment: Cold mountains

Organization: Solitary, pair, or family (1-2 and 2-5 offspring)

Challenge Rating: 12

Treasure: Triple standard

Alignment: Always chaotic evil

Advancement: 22-23 HD (Huge)

Level Adjustment: -

Combat

White dragons prefer sudden assaults, swooping down from aloft or bursting from beneath water, snow, or ice. They loose their breath weapon, then try to knock out a single opponent with a follow-up attack.

Breath WeaponSu

Breath Weapon (Su): A white dragon has one type of breath weapon, a cone of cold.

IcewalkingEx

Icewalking (Ex): This ability works like the spider climb spell, but the surfaces the dragon climbs must be icy. It is always in effect.

Freezing FogSp

Freezing Fog (Sp): An old or older white dragon can use this ability three times per day. It is similar to a solid fog spell but also causes a rime of slippery ice to form on any surface the fog touches, creating the effect of a grease spell. The dragon is immune to the grease effect because of its icewalking ability. This ability is the equivalent of a 5th-level spell.

Other Spell-Like Abilities

Other Spell-Like Abilities: 3/day-fog cloud (juvenile or older), gust of wind (adult or older), wall of ice (ancient or older); 1/day-control weather (great wyrm).

Skills

Skills: Hide, Move Silently, and Swim are considered class skills for white dragons.
