Megaraptor

Huge Animal

Hit Dice: 8d8+43 (79 hp)

Initiative: +2

Speed: 60 ft. (12 squares)

Base Attack/Grapple: +6/+19

Attack: Talons +9 melee (2d8+5)

Full Attack: Talons +9 melee (2d8+5) and 2 foreclaws +4 melee (1d4+2) and bite +4 melee (2d6+2)
