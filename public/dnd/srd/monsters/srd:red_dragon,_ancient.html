Red Dragon, Ancient

Gargantuan Dragon

Hit Dice: 34d12+306 (527 hp)

Initiative: +4

Speed: 40 ft., fly 200 ft. (clumsy)

Armor Class: 39 (-4 size, +33 natural), touch 6, flat-footed 39

Base Attack/Grapple: +34/+60

Attack: Bite +45 (4d6+14) melee

Full Attack: Bite +45 (4d6+14) melee, 2 Claws +45 (2d8+7) melee, 2 Wings +44 (2d6+7) melee, Tail Slap +44 (2d8+21) melee

Space/Reach: 20 ft./15 ft. (20 ft. with bite)

Special Attacks: Breath weapon (60 ft. cone of fire 20d10, DC 36), crush 4d6+21 (DC 36), frightful presence (DC 34), tail sweep 2d6+21 (DC 36)

Special Qualities: Blindsense 60 ft., damage reduction 15/magic, darkvision 120 ft., find the path, immunity to fire, immunity to sleep and paralysis, keen senses, locate object, spell resistance 28, spells (caster level 15th), suggestion, vulnerability to cold

Saves: Fort +28, Ref +19, Will +26

Abilities: Str 39, Dex 10, Con 29, Int 24, Wis 25, Cha 24

Skills: Appraise +39, Bluff +39, Concentration +42, Diplomacy +40, Escape Artist +33, Intimidate +40, Jump +46, Knowledge (any 3) +44, Listen +46, Search +44, Sense Motive +40, Spot +46, Use Magic Device +39

Feats: Alertness, Blind-Fight, Empower Spell, Flyby Attack, Hover, Improved Initiative, Maximize Spell, Power Attack, Snatch, Weapon Focus (bite), Weapon Focus (claw), Wingover

Environment: Warm mountains

Organization: Solitary, pair, or family (1-2 and 2-5 offspring)

Challenge Rating: 23

Treasure: Triple standard

Alignment: Always chaotic evil

Advancement: 35-36 HD (Gargantuan)

Level Adjustment: -

Combat

Because red dragons are so confident, they seldom pause to appraise an adversary. On spotting a target, they make a snap decision whether to attack, using one of many strategies worked out ahead of time. A red dragon lands to attack small, weak creatures with its claws and bite rather than obliterating them with its breath weapon, so as not to destroy any treasure they might be carrying.

Breath WeaponSu

Breath Weapon (Su): A red dragon has one type of breath weapon, a cone of fire.

Locate ObjectSp

Locate Object (Sp): A juvenile or older red dragon can use this ability as the spell of the same name, once per day per age category.

Other Spell-Like Abilities

Other Spell-Like Abilities: 3/day-suggestion (old or older); 1/day-find the path (ancient or older), discern location (great wyrm).

Skills

Skills: Appraise, Bluff, and Jump are considered class skills for red dragons.
