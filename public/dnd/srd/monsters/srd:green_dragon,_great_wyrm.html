Green Dragon, Great wyrm

Gargantuan Dragon

Hit Dice: 38d12+304 (551 hp)

Initiative: +4

Speed: 40 ft., fly 200 ft. (clumsy), swim 40 ft

Armor Class: 43 (-4 size, +37 natural), touch 6, flat-footed 43

Base Attack/Grapple: +38/+64

Attack: Bite +49 (4d6+14) melee

Full Attack: Bite +49 (4d6+14) melee, 2 Claws +49 (2d8+7) melee, 2 Wings +48 (2d6+7) melee, Tail Slap +48 (2d8+21) melee

Space/Reach: 20 ft./15 ft. (20 ft. with bite)

Special Attacks: Breath weapon (60 ft. cone of corrosive (acid) gas 24d6, DC 37), crush 4d6+21 (DC 37), frightful presence (DC 35), tail sweep 2d6+21 (DC 37)

Special Qualities: Blindsense 60 ft., command plants, damage reduction 20/magic, darkvision 120 ft., dominate person, immunity to acid, immunity to sleep and paralysis, keen senses, plant growth, spell resistance 30, spells (caster level 17th), suggestion, water breathing

Saves: Fort +29, Ref +21, Will +27

Abilities: Str 39, Dex 10, Con 27, Int 22, Wis 23, Cha 22

Skills: Bluff +38, Concentration +41, Diplomacy +38, Escape Artist +32, Hide +20, Intimidate +39, Knowledge (any 2) +47, Listen +49, Move Silently +32, Search +47, Sense Motive +39, Spot +49, Use Magic Device +38

Feats: Alertness, Blind-Fight, Cleave, Empower Spell, Flyby Attack, Hover, Improved Initiative, Maximize Spell, Power Attack, Snatch, Weapon Focus (bite), Weapon Focus (claw), Wingover

Environment: Temperate forests

Organization: Solitary, pair, or family (1-2 and 2-5 offspring)

Challenge Rating: 24

Treasure: Triple standard

Alignment: Always lawful evil

Advancement: 39+ HD (Gargantuan)

Level Adjustment: -

Combat

Green dragons initiate fights with little or no provocation, picking on creatures of any size. If the target is intriguing or seems formidable, the dragon stalks the creature to determine the best time to strike and the most appropriate tactics to use. If the target appears weak, the dragon makes its presence known quickly-it enjoys evoking terror. Sometimes the dragon elects to control a humanoid creature through intimidation and suggestion. Green dragons especially like to question adventurers to learn more about their society and abilities, what is going on in the countryside, and if there is treasure nearby.

Breath WeaponSu

Breath Weapon (Su): A green dragon has one type of breath weapon, a cone of corrosive (acid) gas.

Water BreathingEx

Water Breathing (Ex): A green dragon can breathe underwater indefinitely and can freely use its breath weapon, spells, and other abilities while submerged.

Spell-Like Abilities

Spell-Like Abilities: 3/day-suggestion (adult or older), dominate person (ancient or older); 1/day- plant growth (old or older), command plants (great wyrm).

Skills

Skills: Bluff, Hide, and Move Silently are considered class skills for green dragons.
