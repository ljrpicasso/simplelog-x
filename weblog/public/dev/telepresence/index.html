Telepresence

I'm interested in developing a cheap telepresence setup for my own use (since I don't like to travel).

Office of the Future

  • Project at UNC
  • importance of eye contact, also is 3-d
  • traditional videoconferencing does NOT afford eye contact because the camera and the screen are offset, so you are staring at a point (usually) below the other person.
  • video clip (note that it's 3-d using polarized glasses, so it tracks user's head).
  • official? UNC OOTF page
  • National Tele-Immersion Initiative page. Project seems to have ended.
  • Virtually There: Three-dimensional tele-immersion may eventually bring the world to your desk (PDF) in Scientific American. The article that brough eye-contact issues to my attention (it was reprinted in a SciAm special on IT that I just got).
