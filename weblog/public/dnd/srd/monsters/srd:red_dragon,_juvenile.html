Red Dragon, Juvenile

Large Dragon

Hit Dice: 16d12+64 (168 hp)

Initiative: +4

Speed: 40 ft., fly 150 ft. (poor)

Armor Class: 24 (-1 size, +15 natural), touch 9, flat-footed 24

Base Attack/Grapple: +16/+29

Attack: Bite +25 (2d6+9) melee

Full Attack: Bite +25 (2d6+9) melee, 2 Claws +25 (1d8+4) melee, 2 Wings +24 (1d6+4) melee, Tail Slap +24 (1d8+13) melee

Space/Reach: 10 ft./5 ft. (10 ft. with bite)

Special Attacks: Breath weapon (40 ft. cone of fire 8d10, DC 22)

Special Qualities: Blindsense 60 ft., darkvision 120 ft., immunity to fire, immunity to sleep and paralysis, keen senses, locate object, spells (caster level 3rd), vulnerability to cold

Saves: Fort +14, Ref +10, Will +12

Abilities: Str 29, Dex 10, Con 19, Int 14, Wis 15, Cha 14

Skills: Appraise +10, Bluff +10, Concentration +12, Diplomacy +10, Escape Artist +8, Intimidate +10, Jump +17, Knowledge (any 2) +21, Listen +23, Search +21, Sense Motive +10, Spot +23, Use Magic Device +10

Feats: Alertness, Hover, Improved Initiative, Power Attack, Weapon Focus (bite), Weapon Focus (claw)

Environment: Warm mountains

Organization: Solitary or clutch (2-5)

Challenge Rating: 10

Treasure: Triple standard

Alignment: Always chaotic evil

Advancement: 17-18 HD (Large)

Level Adjustment: -

Combat

Because red dragons are so confident, they seldom pause to appraise an adversary. On spotting a target, they make a snap decision whether to attack, using one of many strategies worked out ahead of time. A red dragon lands to attack small, weak creatures with its claws and bite rather than obliterating them with its breath weapon, so as not to destroy any treasure they might be carrying.

Breath WeaponSu

Breath Weapon (Su): A red dragon has one type of breath weapon, a cone of fire.

Locate ObjectSp

Locate Object (Sp): A juvenile or older red dragon can use this ability as the spell of the same name, once per day per age category.

Other Spell-Like Abilities

Other Spell-Like Abilities: 3/day-suggestion (old or older); 1/day-find the path (ancient or older), discern location (great wyrm).

Skills

Skills: Appraise, Bluff, and Jump are considered class skills for red dragons.
