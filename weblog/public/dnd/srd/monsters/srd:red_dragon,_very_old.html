Red Dragon, Very old

Gargantuan Dragon

Hit Dice: 31d12+248 (449 hp)

Initiative: +4

Speed: 40 ft., fly 200 ft. (clumsy)

Armor Class: 36 (-4 size, +30 natural), touch 6, flat-footed 36

Base Attack/Grapple: +31/+56

Attack: Bite +41 (4d6+13) melee

Full Attack: Bite +41 (4d6+13) melee, 2 Claws +41 (2d8+6) melee, 2 Wings +40 (2d6+6) melee, Tail Slap +40 (2d8+19) melee

Space/Reach: 20 ft./15 ft. (20 ft. with bite)

Special Attacks: Breath weapon (60 ft. cone of fire 18d10, DC 33), crush 4d6+19 (DC 33), frightful presence (DC 31), tail sweep 2d6+19 (DC 33)

Special Qualities: Blindsense 60 ft., damage reduction 15/magic, darkvision 120 ft., immunity to fire, immunity to sleep and paralysis, keen senses, locate object, spell resistance 26, spells (caster level 13th), suggestion, vulnerability to cold

Saves: Fort +25, Ref +17, Will +23

Abilities: Str 37, Dex 10, Con 27, Int 22, Wis 23, Cha 22

Skills: Appraise +33, Bluff +33, Concentration +35, Diplomacy +33, Escape Artist +27, Intimidate +33, Jump +39, Knowledge (any 2) +40, Listen +42, Search +40, Sense Motive +33, Spot +42, Use Magic Device +33

Feats: Alertness, Empower Spell, Flyby Attack, Hover, Improved Initiative, Maximize Spell, Power Attack, Snatch, Weapon Focus (bite), Weapon Focus (claw), Wingover

Environment: Warm mountains

Organization: Solitary, pair, or family (1-2 and 2-5 offspring)

Challenge Rating: 21

Treasure: Triple standard

Alignment: Always chaotic evil

Advancement: 32-33 HD (Gargantuan)

Level Adjustment: -

Combat

Because red dragons are so confident, they seldom pause to appraise an adversary. On spotting a target, they make a snap decision whether to attack, using one of many strategies worked out ahead of time. A red dragon lands to attack small, weak creatures with its claws and bite rather than obliterating them with its breath weapon, so as not to destroy any treasure they might be carrying.

Breath WeaponSu

Breath Weapon (Su): A red dragon has one type of breath weapon, a cone of fire.

Locate ObjectSp

Locate Object (Sp): A juvenile or older red dragon can use this ability as the spell of the same name, once per day per age category.

Other Spell-Like Abilities

Other Spell-Like Abilities: 3/day-suggestion (old or older); 1/day-find the path (ancient or older), discern location (great wyrm).

Skills

Skills: Appraise, Bluff, and Jump are considered class skills for red dragons.
