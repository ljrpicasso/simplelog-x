White Dragon, Great wyrm

Gargantuan Dragon

Hit Dice: 36d12+288 (522 hp)

Initiative: +4

Speed: 60 ft., burrow 30 ft., fly 250 ft. (clumsy), swim 60 ft.

Armor Class: 41 (-4 size, +35 natural), touch 6, flat-footed 41

Base Attack/Grapple: +36/+61

Attack: Bite +46 (4d6+13) melee

Full Attack: Bite +46 (4d6+13) melee, 2 Claws +46 (2d8+6) melee, 2 Wings +45 (2d6+6) melee, Tail Slap +45 (2d8+19) melee

Space/Reach: 20 ft./15 ft. (20 ft. with bite)

Special Attacks: Breath weapon (60 ft. cone of cold 12d6, DC 36), crush 4d6+19 (DC 36), frightful presence (DC 32), tail sweep 2d6+19 (DC 36)

Special Qualities: Blindsense 60 ft., control weather, damage reduction 20/magic, darkvision 120 ft., fog cloud, freezing fog, gust of wind, icewalking, immunity to cold, immunity to sleep and paralysis, keen senses, spell resistance 27, spells (caster level 13th), vulnerability to fire, wall of ice

Saves: Fort +28, Ref +20, Will +24

Abilities: Str 37, Dex 10, Con 27, Int 18, Wis 19, Cha 18

Skills: Concentration +32, Diplomacy +28, Escape Artist +24, Hide +12, Intimidate +28, Knowledge (any 2) +43, Listen +45, Move Silently +23, Search +43, Sense Motive +28, Spot +45, Swim +36, Use Magic Device +28

Feats: Alertness, Blind-Fight, Cleave, Empower Spell, Flyby Attack, Hover, Improved Initiative, Maximize Spell, Power Attack, Snatch, Weapon Focus (bite), Weapon Focus (claw), Wingover

Environment: Cold mountains

Organization: Solitary, pair, or family (1-2 and 2-5 offspring)

Challenge Rating: 21

Treasure: Triple standard

Alignment: Always chaotic evil

Advancement: 37+ HD (Gargantuan)

Level Adjustment: -

Combat

White dragons prefer sudden assaults, swooping down from aloft or bursting from beneath water, snow, or ice. They loose their breath weapon, then try to knock out a single opponent with a follow-up attack.

Breath WeaponSu

Breath Weapon (Su): A white dragon has one type of breath weapon, a cone of cold.

IcewalkingEx

Icewalking (Ex): This ability works like the spider climb spell, but the surfaces the dragon climbs must be icy. It is always in effect.

Freezing FogSp

Freezing Fog (Sp): An old or older white dragon can use this ability three times per day. It is similar to a solid fog spell but also causes a rime of slippery ice to form on any surface the fog touches, creating the effect of a grease spell. The dragon is immune to the grease effect because of its icewalking ability. This ability is the equivalent of a 5th-level spell.

Other Spell-Like Abilities

Other Spell-Like Abilities: 3/day-fog cloud (juvenile or older), gust of wind (adult or older), wall of ice (ancient or older); 1/day-control weather (great wyrm).

Skills

Skills: Hide, Move Silently, and Swim are considered class skills for white dragons.
