Ultimate

Blog posts on ultimate

Useful resources for Ultimate (the disc sport...)

Ultimate is a disc sport (we don't use the word "frisbee") played for fun and also competitively. It's a bit of a mix of soccer, football, and basketball, but played with a flying disc. This is a collection of information and resources about the game.

I play with Hamilton Ultimate so these links might be biased towards the local rules.

Ultimate basics

Some starting points.

  • The rules of the game - the UPA rules, 10th edition
  • Ultimate in 10 Simple Rules - for beginners
  • The Ultimate Handbook - a really good overview to the game, with lots of animations
  • Ultimate in Wikipedia - the Wikipedia entry for Ultimate
  • Ultimate Skills - from the Australian federation
  • Disc throws - basics of throwing from Wikipedia
  • BUDA book - looks like distilled wisdom from the Ultimate Handbook, shorter and pithier

Ultimate lingo

There's a lot of special words in ultimate. Don't ask me why. Maybe it's necessary.

  • Ultimate Lingo - a very complete list but only brief explanations
  • Ultimate Slang Dictionary - another one
  • Ultimate Lingo Dictionary - yet another, with fewer words but more detail

Advanced ultimate

  • Ultimate handbook advanced section
  • Ultimate Strategies - from the Toronto ultimate club
  • Ultimate Skills and Drills - a collaborative thing with animations

Ultimate blogs

  • UltimateTalk - Ultimate Talk seems to be the central blog aggregator and chat site for competitive ultimate players in the US
  • Ultimate Frisbee Strategy/Coaching - an interesting blog about coaching and strategy, skills, etc.
  • The Count's blog - Alex de Frondeville from the reknowned "DoG" team in the US
  • Jim Parinella's blog - an outspoken ultimate player from the US team "DoG"

Etc

  • rec.sport.disc - usenet newsgroup dedicated Ultimate
  • Ultimate action shots pool on flickr - photo sharing for ultimate pictures
